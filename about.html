About the Church

The Church of Eigel was established on January 0th 2022*. It was founded to take advantage offer salvation for those who had fallen into depair during the COVID-19 pandemic.

The Church believes in the one true savior Eigel Asinas. According to the ancient scriptures, he was brought to this Earth by a meteor. With his powers, he brings upon miracles to those who believe**.

Note:

* The 0th day of the month is a secret day that only followers of the Church can experience.
** Miracles require a mandatory donation no less than Php 25,000.

About Eigel

Eigel Asinas was born at a very young age. Since then, he has dedicated his life to becoming the best version of himself he could possibly be. That is, a version of himself that can withstand the struggles of life. This led him to formulate the 69 Commandments which build the foundation of our beliefs. He has been toughened by the fires of time and it is possible that he may live forever.

Only time will tell.
